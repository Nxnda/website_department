DEPARTMENT OF IT


The Department of Information Technology at LBS Institute of technology was established in the academic year 1999, offering a four year B.Tech. course in Information Technology.

The Department lays strong emphasis on ensuring that the students have a thorough idea of core concepts. Further, the department focuses on nurturing the analytical abilities of students and exposes them to the latest technologies and equips them with industry-specific skills.

The students have been consistently among the toppers in the University and have gone on to achieve laurels in higher education, research and industry.
